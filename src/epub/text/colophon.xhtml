Colophon

Commentaries on the Gallic War
was published in 58⁠–⁠49 BC by
Julius Caesar.
It was translated from Latin in 1859 by
W. A. McDevitte and W. S. Bohn.

Standard Ebooks
by
Mat Moore,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2004 by
Stan Goodman, Ted Garvin, Carol David, and Distributed Proofreaders
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive.

Vercingetorix Throws Down His Arms at the Feet of Julius Caesar,
a painting completed in 1899 by
Lionel Royer.

League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
